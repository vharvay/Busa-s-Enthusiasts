Emiliano Orgueira

My name is Emiliano Orgueira. I'm an editor and proofreader. I have both a degree in Editing (University of Buenos Aires) and in Proofreading (Fundación Litterae). I work for the National Parliament, at the Proofreading Department for all legislative publications.

I also work as a proofreader for several publishers such as Colihue, Estrada - Macmillan Education, and Pictus. I enjoy all things related to the use of the Spanish language, and I'm always looking for new and exciting ways of applying my knowledge and work experience in different areas.

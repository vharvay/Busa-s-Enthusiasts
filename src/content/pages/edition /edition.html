Our Digital Edition

Our bilingual digital edition of An Account of a Voyage up the River de la Plata, and thence over land to Peru, by Mons. Acarete du Biscay, follows a diplomatic approach that focuses on preserving the original text as it was written, and at the same time uses TEI encoding for a modernize re-reading of the text.
